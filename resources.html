This page is about how you can intervene with the bullying or what to do if you get bullied. There are many ways to intervene without anyone getting hurt. You can make sure the person who is getting bullied is ok or fine because a lot of people are bystanders and they don’t know what to do. We made this website so you can know what to do if this ever happens again. When you are done reading the paragraphs down below, there will be links to the websites in each paragraph.
